Experience

Internship

During the summer of 2022, I got the opportunity to intern as a UX/UI Designer at LINE MAN Wongnai, Thailand's top food delivery platform. I was assigned to the Restaurant Squad that oversees the design of Wongnai Merchant Application used by over half a million merchants all over Thailand.

My 3 months internship involves conducting merchant pain points researches, designing and propose real production projects including Coupon & Promotion management flow, Ads Management, and new Merchant Ranking menu.

Learnings

At LMWN, I got to experienced a fast-paced design process which differs from my previous design experiences. I got to worked with cross-functional teams including project mangers and developers where I learned the importance of design articulation and design iteration when challenged with technicals and business limitations.
