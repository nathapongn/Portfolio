LINE MAN Wongnai Internship

Starting in late May 2022, I was offered the opportunity to intern as a UX Designer at LINE MAN Wongnai and was assigned into the “Restaurant squad” which oversees the design of Wongnai Merchant Application also known as WMA which is used by over half a million restaurants all over Thailand to manage menus, coupons, and deliveries to over a million customers daily through LINE MAN application etc.

Experience

Having only a single UX Design internship experience prior in the finance field, my experience at LINE MAN Wongnai was one of the most memorable, independent and challenging internship where I was given full ownership of several real tasks that actually get implemented and built. I experienced a fast-paced design process, frequent design critique sessions, close communication to product managers & developers where I learned the importance of design alternatives and iterations when faced with limitations.
