LINE MAN Wongnai Internship

Internship

During the summer of 2022, I got the opportunity to intern as a UX/UI Designer at LINE MAN Wongnai, one of Thailand's most popular food delivery platform. I was assigned into the "Restaurant Squad" that oversees the design of Wongnai Merchant Application used by over half a million merchants.

My 3 months internship involves conducting Merchants' pain points research and prototyping & proposal of released projects including Coupon & Promotion management flow, Ads Manager, and new Merchant Rank menu.

Experience

Internship at LINE MAN Wongnai was definitely one of the most memorable, independent and challenging internship where I was given full ownership of several real tasks with actual release plan.

I got to experience a fast-paced design process and working across cross-functional teams including product managers & developers where I learned the importance of clear design communication and design iterations when technical or business limitations arises.
