A little about me

I'm Nat, a UX Designer based in Bangkok with a background in Architecture. Currently designing at SCB (Siam Commercial Bank), one of Thailand's largest financial institutions.

At SCB, I focus primarily on designing business banking platforms that support the financial operations of thousands of companies—from SMEs to the country's largest corporations.

I work across web and mobile on both customer-facing platforms and admin portals. These products help enable some of the most critical financial transactions in Thailand.

What I do

I strive to strike the right balance in designing platforms that support the complex workflows of large corporations while remaining intuitive for our SMEs with simpler needs.

I believe in designing not for ideal behaviors, but for the wonderfully imperfect ways people actually interact with technology.

Outside of work, you'll probably find me at the driving range chasing the perfect swing, cooking up delicious Thai or Italian recipes, or glued to my Kindle.
