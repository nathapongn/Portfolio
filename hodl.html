Context

The Streaming app is Thailand's one and only major stock trading application that was first released back in 2009 and has kept its ticker board style UI aesthetic ever since. (That's almost 13 years ago!)

Live flickering numbers, tickers, and technical numbers, within the bare-bone dark UI design combined with minimal animations, the Streaming app is perhaps the perfect manifestation of what most professional traders would consider the pinnacle of stock trading platforms.

Problem

Although simple at heart and highly informative by looks, new investors could have a very difficult time getting started while some could feel too intimidated to even start especially those who started off exploring by themselves without any background or parental guidance given all the numbers and technical jargon presented at once, all of which combined into a user interface with an overall heavy cognitive load.

Objective

Hodl is a new asset trading platform in Thailand with the objective to simplify access to financial investment especially for inexperienced and casual investors through a simpler, less intimidating and more engaging UI design.

Besides stocks, Hodl will also include other investment options such as cryptocurrency, mutual funds, and oversea stocks all within one uniform platform.

Design Solution

Market Overview

Aside from live numbers and technical statistics, the market overview (Stock Landing Page) page is designed to also include additional information such as the index composition, short About description, and live news with mentions of that specific ticker or index.

These informations can assist both new and experienced investors to help them stay well-informed and conscious of their investment choices before making any investment decision.

Purchase Asset

Purchase & sell feature is designed as a popup bottom sheet with convenient thumb positioning.

The bottom sheet ensures the ticker name and the current price stays unobstructed which ensures that investors are trading the correct asset and adjusting their share price as appropriate when trading an asset with high volatility and rapid share price fluctuation which is very common in Thailand given the small trading volume.

Sell Asset

Cryptocurrency can be sold in a similar manner but investors can choose to sell in either the digital currency or in Thai Baht with the live rate and accurate commission displayed.

Cost Calculation & Fee Transparency

Compared to the two largest crypto (left) & stocks (right) trading platforms in Thailand, commission fees are often excluded from the cost calculation and investors would have to perform the calculation by themselves.

In addition, this could mislead unaware investors and threaten their trust and sense of autonomy over their money if realized much later.

Portfolio

Investors can monitor their portfolio's growth as a line chart or view their portfolio's composition with a ticker-specific colored pie chart.

Unlike other major trading platforms in Thailand, Hodl notifies investors of their pending dividends and stores records of all historical dividends received to ensure an accurate and traceable archive of each investor's cash flow.

Wallet

Hodl wallet acts as a separate bank account that stores funds, receive dividends & profits, and deducts losses. Investors can withdraw and top up instantaneously via their connected bank accounts or debit cards of choice.

With Hodl, investors are not limited to using just their broker's issued bank account like the existing system in Thailand.
