Contextual Problems

1. Most of Us aren't Ready

In 2021, less than 50% of Thais have sufficient savings or investment enough for their retirement according to the NRRI (National Retirement Readiness Index) published by Chulalongkorn University.

This is particularly concerning for the upcoming generation Z and younger due to various reasons including:

  • Increasing ease & ways for frictionless spending
  • Rising cost of living due to lowered restriction in foreign ownership law
  • Increasing shift towards freelance & online jobs with less stable income
  • Lowered attraction towards civil servants with retirement pension
  • Continuous lack of basic financial literacy education in school

2. Limited Options

Since 2009, “Streaming” app has been Thailand's one and only major stock trading application with its infamous ticker board interface design which hasn't changed for over 12 years.

This has worked out fine in the past. But as younger people, those well used & adjusted to new modern globalized platform design language, will eventually become one of the major investors in the near future, Streaming may not be best suited to encourage & educate beginners all while accommodating their higher expectations.

3. It's Easier to Spend

As Thailand continues to rapidly adopts cashless payment, there has been an overwhelming boom of new platforms that continue to introduce more ways for us to spend & borrow money effortlessly.

But fewer to no platforms that encourage people to save & invest for their future selves. While Streaming and other outdated investing platforms continue to thrive through lack of competition.

Pain Points

The 12 Years Old Design

Its UI is perhaps the pinnacle for most professional traders but its ticker board design demands a lot of cognitive loads. It's also not the most engaging for beginners or the most attractive compared to other international platforms.

Uninformative

Investing abbreviations are used all around the platform without any explaination provided. So investors have to rely on external sources while some turn to social media for help and risk receiving the wrong or misleading information.

Care Free

Unfortunately, stock manipulation is a daily occurrence in SET. Streaming's lack of warnings and its abbreviations does little to remind investors to stay cautious and have second thoughts before opening a trade.

Steep Cost

The inconvenience of odd lot orders and the minimum of 100 shares required for each buy/sell order on Streaming makes large-cap stocks with lower volatility inaccessible to some beginners and investors with low starting capital.

Limited International Options

Streaming has long been limited to Thai stocks and all available international stocks are in a form of Depositary Receipt (DR).

Design Solution

Flint is an asset investing app concept designed with a fresh new look to attract young audiences to begin investing. Flint aims to tackle existing pain points of the current monopolized app by providing a one-stop platform with informative & friendly design, broader investment opportunities, lower cost barriers, and improved conveniences.

Customizable Home

No longer fixed to SET Index, the Home screen is designed to be customizable to match each user's investing styles & interests.

Flint's "For You" section reminds investors of relevant upcoming events in a calendar view specific to their portfolio holdings

Ticker

Complete with all the essential information specific to each ticker including market status, basic statistics, earnings charts, company info, and index composition. Helping investors stay well informed.

Informative Design

Define

For all statistics label

Inform

For special type assets (ex. RMF, SSF, LTF, Warrent, Derivative Warrent, REITs, ETF, Leverage, AltCoin)

Warning

For assets with high volatility / low volume / unusual trending etc;

Alert

For assets with Market Surveillance Alert (SET Index)

Purchase & Sell

Flint offers various purchase & sell methods allowing investors of all levels to invest without the 100 shares minimum or the hassle of odd lots, minimizing cost barriers and making lessons less expensive.

Watchlist

Conveniently add your favorite tickers right from the detail page to your customizable Watchlists.

Portfolio

Investors can view both the composition and progress of their portfolio across different asset types.

Wallet

Similar to a conventional e-saving account, Flint allows investors to swiftly deposit, transfer, and exchange right from their wallet without having to wait 2-3 business days to move money around.

Pay

Additionally, Flint Debit let investors pay using their wallet's balance without the hassle of moving money around to a different account when needed.
