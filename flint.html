Contexts

One App Rules All

Since 2009, the "Streaming" app has been Thailand's only major stock trading platform, featuring an unchanged ticker-style interface for over 12 years. While it may works for its current user base, it may not appeal to future potential younger investors.

Streaming caters to its wide experienced user base but offers little to no information to educate and protect those with less experience. Plain abbreviations and jargons are widely used making it challenging for newcomers. Additionally, Streaming has very limited international investment options.

No Competition

The rapid adoption of cashless payment has sparked a surge in new platforms offering convenient spending and borrowing options. However, the investing platform market lacks real competition with little to no new players introduced in the past years.

Design Solution

Flint is an investing app concept aimed at attracting young users. With its fresh and appealing design, Flint addresses the pain points of the existing monopolized app by offering a one-stop platform that is informative, user-friendly, and visually engaging. It provides a wide range of investment opportunities, reduces cost barriers, and enhances convenience, making investing accessible and enjoyable for all.

Customizable

No longer fixed to SET Index, the Home screen is designed to be customizable to match each user's investing styles & interests.

Flint's "For You" section reminds investors of relevant upcoming events in a calendar view specific to their portfolio holdings

Ticker

Complete with all the essential information specific to each ticker including market status, basic statistics, earnings charts, company info, and index composition. Helping investors stay well informed.

Purchase & Sell

Flint offers various purchase & sell methods allowing investors of all levels to invest without the 100 shares minimum or the hassle of odd lots, minimizing cost barriers and making lessons less expensive.

Watchlist

Conveniently add your favorite tickers right from the detail page to your customizable Watchlists.

Portfolio

Investors can view both the composition and progress of their portfolio across different asset types.

Wallet

Similar to a conventional e-saving account, Flint allows investors to swiftly deposit, transfer, and exchange right from their wallet without having to wait 2-3 business days to move money around.

Pay

Additionally, Flint Debit let investors pay using their wallet's balance without the hassle of moving money around to a different account when needed.
