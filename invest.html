Simplify Your Financial Journey

Save, Spend & Invest. All in One App.

One App Rules

Context

“Streaming” has long dominated as the sole stock investing app in Thailand since 2009. While its overwhelming ticker board interface and basic functionalities may appeal to parts of its experienced user base, they pose challenges for beginners due to their overly straightforward nature.

Numerous pain points such as trading jargon, an uninformative interface, a slow deposit/withdrawal process, and a lack of personalization make the experience daunting for beginners.

All-in-One

Objective

The goal is to design 'Flint,' an investing app that makes investing less intimidating by providing an informative and educational experience through a modern user-friendly interface. Flint aims to seamlessly integrate investing, savings, and spending into one convenient platform while ensuring accessibility by reducing cost barriers and offering extensive international investment opportunities.

Ideation

I began visualizing my idea by sketching up wireframes of important menus, utilizing sets of keywords to define the core functions and features that I plan to include.

All About You

Home

Stay informed about your investments, track goals, view a personalized dividend calendar, and catch up on the latest articles—all in a distraction-free environment with only the information relevant to you.

Accessible

Investing

Odd lot purchase (orders of less than 100 shares) is integrated as part of Thai stocks investing to make highly priced large cap stocks more accessible to new investors. This reduces the risk of investors having to stretch beyond their risk tolerance or being compelled to invest in lower priced stocks that are often more volatile.

Insightful

Securities Information

By simply tapping on the text, information bottom sheets offer concise explanations for trading jargon and investing metric. This keeps investors informed and reduces the need to rely on external sources.

Informative

Alerts

Bottom sheets automatically appear when users enter securities with trading alerts or critical information to ensure investors awareness and understanding.

Flagged Securities

Tax Benefit Funds

Leveraged Funds

Instant

Wallet

Just like a common savings account, users can deposit and withdraw funds instantaneously, scan QR to pay, transfer to other accounts, and exchange currency all within one app. Users will no longer have to wait 2-3 business days to move available balance from their wallet.

Convenience

Cash Card

Flint's cash card provides a hassle-free way to spend or withdraw cash from an ATM directly from the wallet. Real-time access to balances makes deposit commitments less intimidating for beginners.

Key Takeaways

This project allowed me to envision a platform that promotes the idea that investing should be more accessible with lower cost, skill, and knowledge barriers. The resulting vision ended up becoming more than just an investing platform but also an app that unites payment, budgeting, and investing into one.

During the design process, I was able to practice designing for mobile, building a design system from scratch, and applying transitions in prototypes for an enhanced user experience.
