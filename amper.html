Problem

Fragmented Networks

As electric vehicles rapidly gain popularity in Thailand, so do the expansion of public EV chargers. But with growth comes fragmentation. Each charging provider operates its own app, leading many EV users to juggle multiple apps just to stay charged.

With each provider comes a different interface, user journey, and payment method—forcing drivers to constantly relearn how to charge depending on where they go.

For many EV drivers, the biggest obstacle isn't finding a charger—it's navigating the messy ecosystem around it.

Special Cases with Extra Steps

Risks of uninformative interface

Insufficient information leads to over-reliance on external information which then leads to misinformation and bias.

Objective

Make investing less intimidating by providing an informative investing environment through a modern and inviting user-friendly interface with new added features to enhance convenience.

Discoverability

Reducing the reliance on external sources of information empowers users to make well-informed choices.

Design Solution

Personalized

Home

A distraction-free environment that allows users to stay on top of their investments, track goals, read the latest articles to stay informed, and view upcoming events such as dividend payout dates, economic data releases, and earnings reports, all personalized to their holdings and watchlists.

Accessible

Investing

Odd lot purchases (orders of fewer than 100 shares) are integrated into Thai stock investing. Enhancing the transparency of the odd lot feature makes high-priced largecap stocks more accessible and reduces the risk of investors feeling compelled to invest in lower-priced, often more volatile, stocks.

Insightful

Securities Information

To enhance discoverability, each security includes extensive statistic and financial informatiom. Additionally, trading jargon, abbreviations, and symbols are accompanied by informative bottom sheets that open with a tap, ensuring investors stay well-informed and lowering the need for external sources.

Alert Bottom Sheet

Automatically opens when users enter a security that requires additional acknowledgment.

Market Surveillance Alert

Tax Benefit Fund (SSF & RMF)

High Risk Equity

Instantaneous

Wallet

Like a regular savings account, users can invest directly from their wallet's available balance or spend from their cash balance via bank transfer, QR payment, and cash card. Eliminating the 1-2 business day withdrawal process of a typical brokerage account.

Convenient

Cash Card

Flint's cash card provides a hassle-free way to spend or withdraw cash from an ATM directly from the THB cash balance. Real-time access to the cash balance makes deposit commitments less intimidating for beginners.

Style Guide

Reflections

This project was inspired by challenges I faced when starting my investing journey, along with similar experiences shared by others in social media investment groups.

Initially envisioned as a smaller project, further research revealed more areas for improvement, leading to expanded solutions beyond UI enhancements. As the scope grew, I learned to build a comprehensive design system from scratch to ensure consistency and integrated motion design to further enhance the user experience.
